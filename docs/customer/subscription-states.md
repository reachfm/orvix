# Understanding Subscription States

Your Orvix subscription can be in one of several states. Each state determines what features are available and what you should do next.

## Subscription States

| State | Description | What You Can Do |
| ----- | ----------- | --------------- |
| **Trial** | You are in the 14-day free trial. | All features available. Add a payment method before the trial ends. |
| **Active** | Your subscription is current and paid. | All features available. |
| **Past Due** | A payment has failed or is overdue. | Services continue for a grace period. Update your payment method. |
| **Suspended** | Payment is significantly overdue or trial has ended. | Email service is stopped. Mail flow is halted. Data is preserved. |
| **Canceled** | Subscription has been canceled. | Services stopped. Data preserved for 30 days. |
| **Deleted** | Organization has been permanently removed. | All data destroyed. Cannot be recovered. |

## Grace Periods

- **Payment failure**: 7-day grace period to resolve the issue before services are affected.
- **Past due**: 14 days after the due date before suspension.
- **Post-cancellation**: 30 days before permanent data deletion.
- **Post-trial**: 30 days after trial ends before data is deleted.

## Resolving Past Due

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Review the outstanding invoice.
3. Update your payment method if needed.
4. Click **Pay Now** to settle the balance.

Services resume immediately after payment is processed.

## During Suspension

While your subscription is suspended:

- Incoming email is rejected (senders receive a bounce notification).
- Outgoing email is blocked.
- You can still sign in and access billing to resolve the issue.
- Mailbox data is preserved for 30 days.

## Canceling Your Subscription

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. Provide a reason for cancellation (optional, helps us improve).
4. Click **Confirm Cancellation**.

Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, the canceled state begins.

## Reactivating a Canceled Subscription

1. Sign in to your Orvix account within 30 days of cancellation.
2. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
3. Click **Reactivate Subscription**.
4. Enter your payment details.
5. Click **Reactivate**.

All data is restored. If more than 30 days have passed since cancellation, data cannot be recovered.
