# Creating an Orvix Account

## Sign Up

1. Go to [https://app.orvix.com/signup](https://app.orvix.com/signup).
2. Enter your email address and choose a password.
3. Click **Create Account**.
4. Check your inbox for a verification email. Click the link inside to confirm your email address.
5. Once verified, you will be prompted to create your organization and add your first domain.

## Sign In

1. Go to [https://app.orvix.com/login](https://app.orvix.com/login).
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. If MFA is enabled, enter your one-time code.
4. Click **Sign In**.

## Sign In with SSO

If your organization has SSO configured:

1. Go to [https://app.orvix.com/login](https://app.orvix.com/login).
2. Click **Sign in with SSO**.
3. Enter your organization's domain and click **Continue**.
4. You will be redirected to your identity provider to authenticate.

## Sign Out

Click your profile icon in the top-right corner and select **Sign Out**.
