# How to Contact Support

Orvix provides support through multiple channels depending on your plan.

## Support Channels

| Channel | Availability | Best For |
| ------- | ------------ | -------- |
| **Knowledge Base** | All plans, 24/7 | Self-service answers |
| **Email Support** | All plans | Non-urgent issues |
| **Live Chat** | Professional and above | Quick questions during business hours |
| **Priority Support** | Business and Enterprise | Faster response times |
| **Dedicated Account Manager** | Enterprise | Account-specific needs |
| **Phone Support** | Enterprise | Critical issues |

## Email Support

Send your question to the appropriate address:

| Type | Address |
| ---- | ------- |
| General support | support@orvix.com |
| Billing questions | billing@orvix.com |
| Sales inquiries | sales@orvix.com |
| Security issues | security@orvix.com |
| Abuse reports | abuse@orvix.com |

## Live Chat

1. Sign in to your Orvix dashboard.
2. Click the chat icon in the bottom-right corner.
3. Describe your issue and a support agent will respond.

Live chat is available Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–6:00 PM UTC. For Professional plan users, chat is available during business hours. Business plan users have extended chat hours.

## What to Include in Your Message

To help us resolve your issue quickly, include:

- Your organization name and domain.
- A clear description of the issue.
- Steps you have already taken to troubleshoot.
- Any error messages or screenshots.
- The email addresses involved (if applicable).
- The date and time the issue started.

## Response Times

| Plan | Initial Response Time |
| ---- | --------------------: |
| Starter | Within 24 hours |
| Professional | Within 8 hours |
| Business | Within 2 hours |
| Enterprise | Within 30 minutes (critical) |

Response times are for business hours unless your plan includes 24/7 support.

## Filing an Urgent Ticket

For time-sensitive issues:

1. Tag your email subject with `[URGENT]`.
2. Provide a phone number for callbacks if needed.
3. Select the appropriate priority level if using the support portal.

## Status Page

Before contacting support, check the [Orvix status page](status.md) to see if there is an ongoing service incident. Known outages are posted there with updates.

## Feature Requests

We welcome feedback. Submit feature requests to feedback@orvix.com or use the **Feedback** option in the dashboard help menu.
