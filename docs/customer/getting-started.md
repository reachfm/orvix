# Getting Started with Orvix

Welcome to Orvix Enterprise Mail - a managed email hosting service.

## Quick Start

1. **Create an account** at app.orvix.email/signup
2. **Create your organization** - this is your company workspace
3. **Invite your team** - add members as administrators or users
4. **Add your domain** - prove ownership via DNS TXT record
5. **Configure mail DNS** - set up MX, SPF, DKIM, DMARC records
6. **Create mailboxes** - add mailboxes for your users
7. **Start sending and receiving** - use Orvix Webmail or any email client

## Account Creation

Visit app.orvix.email/signup and enter your email address and password.
You will receive a verification email to confirm your account.

## Organization

After signing in, create your organization. This is the workspace
where you manage domains, mailboxes, billing, and team members.
