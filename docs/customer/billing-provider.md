# Billing Provider Status

Orvix partners with a third-party billing provider to handle payments, invoicing, and subscription management securely.

## Billing Provider Dashboard

Your billing provider account is automatically created when you add a payment method. You can manage payment methods and view invoices from the Orvix dashboard or directly through the billing provider.

## Accessing Your Billing Account

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Under the billing provider section, you can see your billing account status, payment method, and billing history.
3. To manage billing details directly, click the link to the billing provider portal.

## Adding a Payment Method

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Click **Add Payment Method**.
3. Enter your card details or select an alternative payment method if available in your region.
4. Click **Save**.

Your payment method is stored securely by the billing provider. Orvix never has access to your full card number.

## Updating Your Payment Method

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Click **Update** next to your payment method.
3. Enter the new card details.
4. Click **Save**.

## Accepted Payment Methods

| Method | Availability |
| ------ | ------------ |
| Credit / Debit Card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) | All regions |
| SEPA Direct Debit | Europe |
| ACH | United States |
| Wire Transfer | Enterprise plans only |

## Invoices and Receipts

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing** > **Invoices**.
2. View and download PDF invoices for each billing period.
3. Each invoice includes the billing provider's tax ID, your company details, and a line-item breakdown.

## Billing Information

Update your billing address and tax ID:

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing** > **Billing Information**.
2. Edit the company name, address, and VAT/GST number if applicable.
3. Click **Save**.

This information appears on all future invoices.

## Common Issues

### Payment Failed

If a payment fails, check that:
- Your card has sufficient funds.
- The card has not expired.
- Your bank allows international transactions (if applicable).
- 3D Secure authentication is completed if prompted.

Update your payment method and retry from **Organization** > **Billing**.

### Billing Provider Service Disruption

In the rare event that the billing provider experiences an outage:
- Your email service continues without interruption.
- Payment processing may be delayed. You will not be suspended during a known billing provider outage.
- Check the [Orvix status page](status.md) for updates.

### Disputed Charges

If you believe a charge is incorrect, contact [Orvix support](support.md) before disputing with your bank. Disputes may result in immediate suspension of your account.
