# Transferring Organization Ownership

Organization ownership can be transferred from one member to another. Only the current owner can initiate a transfer.

## Prerequisites

- You must be the current **Owner** of the organization.
- The recipient must already be a member of your organization.
- The recipient must have MFA enabled on their account.

## Initiating a Transfer

1. Go to **Organization** > **Settings** > **Danger Zone**.
2. Click **Transfer Ownership**.
3. Select the member you want to transfer ownership to from the dropdown.
4. Enter your password to confirm.
5. Click **Initiate Transfer**.

## Accepting or Rejecting the Transfer

The recipient will receive an email notification:

1. They must click **Accept Transfer** in the email within 7 days.
2. If they reject or do not respond within 7 days, the transfer is canceled.

## What Happens After Transfer

- The previous owner's role changes from **Owner** to **Admin**.
- The new owner gains full control, including the ability to transfer ownership again or delete the organization.
- Billing responsibility transfers to the new owner.
- All other member roles and settings remain unchanged.

## Canceling a Transfer

Before the recipient accepts, you can cancel the transfer:

1. Go to **Organization** > **Settings** > **Danger Zone**.
2. Click **Cancel Transfer**.
