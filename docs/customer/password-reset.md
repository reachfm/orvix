# Resetting Your Password

If you have forgotten your password or need to change it, follow the steps below.

## Forgot Your Password

1. Go to [https://app.orvix.com/login](https://app.orvix.com/login).
2. Click **Forgot password?** below the sign-in form.
3. Enter the email address associated with your Orvix account.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.
5. Check your inbox for the password reset email. If you don't see it, check your spam or junk folder.
6. Click the link in the email. The link expires after 1 hour.
7. Enter your new password. It must be at least 8 characters long and include a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
8. Click **Reset Password**.

## Change Your Password While Signed In

1. Click your profile icon in the top-right corner.
2. Select **Account Settings**.
3. Go to the **Password** tab.
4. Enter your current password.
5. Enter your new password (twice for confirmation).
6. Click **Change Password**.

## Best Practices

- Use a unique password you do not use on other sites.
- Consider enabling [multi-factor authentication](mfa.md) for added security.
- Use a password manager to generate and store strong passwords.
