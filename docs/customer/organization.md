# Creating and Managing Your Organization

An organization in Orvix represents your company or team. It contains your domains, mailboxes, members, and billing.

## Creating an Organization

When you first sign up, you will be prompted to create an organization:

1. Enter your organization name (typically your company name).
2. Enter your first domain (e.g., `example.com`).
3. Click **Create Organization**.

If you need to create additional organizations, contact support.

## Managing Organization Settings

Navigate to **Organization** > **Settings** to configure:

| Setting | Description |
| ------- | ----------- |
| **Name** | Your organization display name. |
| **Logo** | Upload a logo for your webmail interface. |
| **Default Language** | Interface language for new users. |
| **Timezone** | Default timezone for mail timestamps and reports. |

## Switching Organizations

If you belong to multiple organizations:

1. Click the organization name in the top-left corner.
2. Select the organization you want to switch to.

## Deleting Your Organization

Deleting an organization permanently removes all domains, mailboxes, and data. This action cannot be undone.

1. Go to **Organization** > **Settings** > **Danger Zone**.
2. Click **Delete Organization**.
3. Enter the organization name to confirm.
4. Click **Delete Permanently**.

## Prerequisites for Deletion

Before an organization can be deleted:

- All mailboxes must be removed.
- All domains must be removed.
- The subscription must be canceled.
- All outstanding invoices must be paid.
