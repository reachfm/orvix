## Viewing Your Current Plan

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Your current plan is displayed at the top of the page.

## Switching Plans

### Upgrading

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Select the plan you want to upgrade to.
4. Review the prorated cost (you are charged only the difference for the remainder of your billing cycle).
5. Click **Confirm Upgrade**.

The upgrade takes effect immediately. All features of the new plan are available right away.

### Downgrading

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Select a lower-tier plan.
4. Review the changes — downgrading may require removing resources that exceed the new plan's limits.
5. Click **Confirm Downgrade**.

Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle. You continue on your current plan until then.

### Switching Billing Cycle

1. Go to **Organization** > **Billing**.
2. Click **Change Cycle**.
3. Choose **Monthly** or **Annual**.
4. Review the pricing change (annual plans receive a discount).
5. Click **Confirm**.

## Free Trial

New organizations start on a 14-day free trial of the Professional plan. No credit card is required. At the end of the trial:

- You must add a payment method and choose a plan to continue using Orvix.
- If no action is taken, your organization is suspended (data is preserved for 30 days).
