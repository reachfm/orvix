# Data and Privacy Overview

This page explains how Orvix handles your data, what data is collected, and your privacy options.

## Data We Process

As an email hosting platform, Orvix processes the following data on your behalf:

- **Account data**: Email addresses, names, organization details, and billing information.
- **Email content**: The contents of messages sent and received through your mailboxes.
- **Metadata**: Sender, recipient, timestamps, IP addresses, and email headers.
- **Usage data**: Volume of messages, storage consumed, and feature usage.
- **DNS records**: Domain configuration data you provide for verification and routing.

## How We Use Data

- **To provide the service**: Routing, delivering, and storing email; verifying domains; managing mailboxes.
- **To ensure security**: Spam filtering, virus scanning, abuse prevention, and threat detection.
- **To improve the service**: Aggregated and anonymized usage analytics.
- **To bill and communicate**: Invoicing, service notifications, and support.

## What We Do Not Do

- Orvix does **not** scan email content for advertising purposes.
- Orvix does **not** sell your data to third parties.
- Orvix does **not** use your data to train AI/ML models without explicit opt-in.

## Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention |
| --------- | --------- |
| Email messages | Retained for the life of the mailbox. Deleted messages are purged from Trash after 30 days. |
| Account information | Retained while your account is active and up to 90 days after cancellation. |
| Billing records | Retained for 7 years per financial regulations. |
| Logs and metadata | Retained for up to 90 days for operational purposes. |
| Backups | Daily backups retained for 30 days. |

## Your Data Controls

| Feature | Where to Configure |
| ------- | ----------------- |
| Delete your account | **Account Settings** > **Danger Zone** |
| Delete a mailbox | **Domains** > your domain > **Mailboxes** |
| Export your data | Contact support — available for all plans. |
| Data processing preferences | **Account Settings** > **Privacy** |

## GDPR and Data Protection

Orvix complies with GDPR. Key information:

- Data is processed in data centers in the US and EU. Enterprise customers may choose a specific region.
- Sub-processors are listed at [https://orvix.com/dpa](https://orvix.com/dpa).
- A Data Processing Agreement (DPA) is available for Business and Enterprise plans upon request.
- Data Subject Access Requests (DSARs) can be submitted to privacy@orvix.com.

## Data Encryption

- **In transit**: All connections use TLS 1.2 or higher.
- **At rest**: All stored data is encrypted using AES-256.
- **Backups**: Encrypted with the same standard.

## Security Certifications

Orvix maintains SOC 2 Type II certification. Compliance reports are available to Enterprise customers under NDA.

## Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact privacy@orvix.com. Our full Privacy Policy is at [https://orvix.com/privacy](https://orvix.com/privacy).
