# Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

Multi-factor authentication protects your Orvix account by requiring a second verification step when you sign in.

## Enabling MFA

1. Sign in to your Orvix account.
2. Go to **Account Settings** > **Security** > **Multi-Factor Authentication**.
3. Click **Set Up MFA**.
4. Choose your preferred method:

### Authenticator App (Recommended)

1. Select **Authenticator App** and click **Continue**.
2. Open your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, 1Password, etc.) on your phone.
3. Scan the QR code displayed on screen, or manually enter the setup key.
4. Enter the 6-digit code shown in your app to verify.
5. Click **Confirm**.

### Security Key

1. Select **Security Key** and click **Continue**.
2. Insert your hardware security key (YubiKey, etc.) into a USB port.
3. Touch the key when prompted.
4. Name your key for identification and click **Save**.

## Using MFA

After enabling MFA, you will be prompted for your second factor every time you sign in:

- **Authenticator app**: Open the app and enter the current 6-digit code.
- **Security key**: Insert or tap your key when prompted.

## Recovery Codes

When you enable MFA, Orvix generates a set of 10 recovery codes. Each code can be used once to sign in if you lose access to your MFA device.

- **Store recovery codes securely** — treat them like passwords.
- To view or regenerate codes, go to **Account Settings** > **Security** > **MFA** > **Recovery Codes**.

## Disabling MFA

1. Go to **Account Settings** > **Security** > **Multi-Factor Authentication**.
2. Click **Disable MFA**.
3. Enter your password to confirm.

## Lost Access to MFA?

If you have lost your MFA device and do not have recovery codes, contact [Orvix support](support.md). Identity verification will be required before MFA can be removed from your account.
