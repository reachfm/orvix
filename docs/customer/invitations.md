# Inviting Team Members

Invite team members to join your Orvix organization so they can manage domains, mailboxes, and settings.

## Sending an Invitation

1. Go to **Organization** > **Members**.
2. Click **Invite Member**.
3. Enter the email address of the person you want to invite.
4. Select a [role](roles.md) from the dropdown.
5. Optionally, select which domains this person should have access to.
6. Click **Send Invitation**.

The invitee will receive an email with a link to join your organization.

## Accepting an Invitation

If you received an invitation:

1. Check your inbox for the Orvix invitation email.
2. Click **Accept Invitation** in the email.
3. If you already have an Orvix account, sign in and the organization will be added to your account.
4. If you do not have an Orvix account, you will be prompted to create one.

## Managing Pending Invitations

To view or cancel pending invitations:

1. Go to **Organization** > **Members**.
2. Click the **Pending** tab.
3. To cancel an invitation, click the three-dot menu and select **Cancel Invitation**.

## Resending an Invitation

1. Go to **Organization** > **Members** > **Pending**.
2. Click the three-dot menu next to the invitation.
3. Select **Resend Invitation**.

## Removing a Member

1. Go to **Organization** > **Members**.
2. Find the member you want to remove.
3. Click the three-dot menu and select **Remove Member**.
4. Confirm the removal.

Removing a member revokes their access to the organization immediately. It does not delete their Orvix account.
